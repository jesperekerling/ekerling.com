The Story

Once upon a time, on a dark night in front of a computer screen in 1995, a young boy discovered Internet for the first time. This boy realized that everyone with Internet could read what someone else wrote. The boy realized that the media lanscape would be changed forever. Media content would be created by the people, not only the big old media companies. Things would get personal, people with the same interests would be able to connect in serveral ways. This thing (the Internet) was an opptunity to create a better world and to help people to connect. After a week or two this boy started to create his own website.
